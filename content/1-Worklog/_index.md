**On this page**, you will need to introduce your worklog. **How** did you complete it? How many weeks did you take to complete the program? **What** did you do in those weeks?

Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 3 months (throughout the internship period) with weekly contents as follows:

**Week 1:** [FCJ onboarding, AWS Console & CLI, EC2 foundations — 20–26 Apr 2026](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [FCJ, AWS services, Console/CLI & EC2 — 27 Apr–3 May 2026](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [CloudWatch · Support · Route 53 · CLI · IAM Identity Center — 4–8 May 2026](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [AWS Backup · VM Import/Export · Docker · ECS · CI/CD — 11–15 May 2026](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [Security Hub · VPC Peering · Transit Gateway · Lambda · CodePipeline — 18–22 May 2026](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [Storage Gateway · FSx · WAF · Tags · IAM — 25–29 May 2026](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [Grafana · IAM Boundary · Systems Manager · KMS · Data Lake — 1–5 Jun 2026](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [Data Lake · CloudFormation · CDK · DynamoDB · Migration — 8–12 Jun 2026](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9:** [IAM Role · Lightsail · Step Functions — 15–19 Jun 2026](1.9-week9/)

**Week 10:** [Cloud9 · Microservices · Kinesis — 22–26 Jun 2026](1.10-week10/)

**Week 11:** [DACSWEBSK deployment — VPC · RDS · S3 · EC2 · Lambda — 29 Jun–3 Jul 2026](1.11-week11/)

**Week 12:** [DACSWEBSK finalize & testing — Monitoring · Workshop · Report — 6–9 Jul 2026](1.12-week12/)
