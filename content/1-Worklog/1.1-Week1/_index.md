**Week 1:** 20 April – 24 April 2026

### Week 1 Objectives

- Get familiar with the First Cloud Journey (FCJ) environment and program members.
- Understand the overall AWS architecture and main service categories.
- Create and activate an AWS Free Tier account for learning.
- Explore AWS learning paths and resources from AWS Study Group.
- Practice identity and access management with AWS Identity and Access Management (IAM).

### Week 1 Outcomes

- Familiar with the FCJ learning environment, understood rules, and connected with team members.
- Understood AWS overall architecture and core service groups: Compute, Storage, Networking, and Database.
- Completed AWS Free Tier registration and verification, successfully resolving phone verification issues via AWS Support.
- Successfully activated 200 USD AWS Promotional Credits for hands-on AWS practice throughout the program.
- Understood AWS learning paths, AWS Study Group resources, and reference architectures on AWS.
- Successfully practiced IAM: creating and managing Users, Groups, Roles, and access permissions with Least Privilege.
- Understood the importance of identity and access management for AWS security and safety.
