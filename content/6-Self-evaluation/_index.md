During my internship at **Amazon Web Services Vietnam** from **20 April 2026** to **12 July 2026**, I had the opportunity to learn, practice, and apply knowledge from school in a real working environment.

I participated in the **Event Management (DACSWEBSK)** project — deploying an ASP.NET Core 8 MVC application on AWS — improving skills in **cloud architecture design, AWS Console/CLI usage, and integrating services (EC2, RDS, S3, SES, Lambda, ALB, WAF)**, writing Workshop documentation, and preparing internship reports. I also completed FCJ labs, attended FCAJ community events, and attended translated AWS technical blogs.

Regarding work ethic, I followed FCJ program schedules and processes, researched materials before hands-on practice, and actively collaborated with my mentor and **Fix Later** team to complete tasks on time. I assess myself as **fairly strong** in most criteria; areas such as deeper professional knowledge, proactiveness, and technical communication still have room for growth.

### Needs Improvement

- **Professional knowledge:** Deepen cloud architecture and advanced AWS services (IaC, security, cost optimization) to move from fair toward stronger proficiency.
- **Proactiveness:** Research and experiment more independently before seeking help, especially with deployment errors or complex configurations.
- **Sense of responsibility:** Plan long phases (workshop, testing) in more detail to meet deadlines without last-minute rushing.
- **Communication:** Present technical ideas and progress reports more concisely and structurally with mentors and teammates.
- **Problem-solving:** Practice a more systematic approach (root cause analysis, layer-by-layer infrastructure checks) when troubleshooting AWS issues.
